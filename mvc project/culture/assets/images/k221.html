CURATED ART AND CRAFT CLASSES FOR ADULTS AND CHILDREN​

We offer a wide array of art classes and craft classes for children and adults.​​

Painting classes

Classes are conducted for children of age 12 years and above and adults in various forms of painting. Both basic and advanced courses are available.

2:30 p.m. to 6:30 p.m (Saturday)

Raja Rajeshwari Nagar:

Timings: 11:00 a.m.. to 4:00 p.m.

Days: Monday to Saturday

Different types of painting classes conducted are:​

*Acrylic painting

*Oil painting

*Glass panting (Stained glass and Reverse glass)

*Charcoal painting

*Fabric painting

*Pot painting

*Nib painting

*Tanjore painting

*3-D cold ceramic painting
